Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2025

Privacy-First Approach: UtiliMax is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data and store most information locally on your device. No personal information like email, name, or phone number is collected.

Welcome to UtiliMax (All in One Tools). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application. Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the application.

1. Information We Collect

We believe in minimal data collection. Here's exactly what our app does and doesn't collect:

What We DO Collect:

  • Ad Interaction Data: When you interact with advertisements (clicks, impressions), this data is collected by Google AdMob for advertising purposes.
  • App Preferences: Your app settings (theme preference, language, tool configurations) are stored locally on your device.
  • Tool Usage History: QR codes you generate/scan and conversion history are stored locally on your device for your convenience.
  • Device Capabilities: We check if your device has a camera and flash for the mirror and flashlight tools to function properly.

What We DO NOT Collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Analytics or usage tracking data
  • Crash reports or performance data
  • Photos or images (camera is preview-only)
  • Audio recordings
  • Contact information
  • Device fingerprinting data
  • Browsing history or network activity
  • File system access beyond app data

Important: Firebase Analytics is explicitly disabled in our app. We do not track your app usage or collect analytics data.

2. How We Use Your Information

The minimal data we collect is used solely for:

  • App Functionality: To provide the tools and features you expect (calculator, converters, QR tools, etc.)
  • User Experience: To remember your preferences and settings locally
  • Tool History: To provide convenient access to your recent QR codes and conversions
  • Advertising: To display relevant ads through Google AdMob (in test mode)

We do NOT use your information for:

  • User profiling or tracking
  • Marketing communications
  • Data analysis or insights
  • Selling to third parties

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage: All your personal data (preferences, history, settings) is stored locally on your device using secure local databases. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly delete and reinstall the app.

Security: We implement appropriate security measures to protect your locally stored data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party services with limited data sharing:

Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. Currently, our app is in test mode using test ad units. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and interaction data according to Google's Privacy Policy. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

Firebase Remote Config

We use Firebase Remote Config only for app configuration (like ad settings). No personal data is shared with Firebase, and Firebase Analytics is explicitly disabled.

Camera Access

The app requests camera permission for the Mirror tool and QR code scanner. The camera feed is only displayed in real-time and never stored, recorded, or transmitted.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since we don't collect personal data, there are no special privacy concerns for children using this app.

6. Your Data Control

Since most data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Clear App Data: You can clear all locally stored data through your device's app settings or our app's clear data feature.
  • Uninstall: Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data.
  • No Account Deletion Needed: Since we don't store your data on our servers, there are no accounts to delete.

7. Permissions Explained

Our app requests these permissions for functionality only:

  • Camera: For Mirror tool and QR code scanning (no data stored or transmitted)
  • Internet: For displaying ads and fetching app configuration
  • Storage: For saving your preferences and tool history locally

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Since we collect minimal data, privacy policy changes are unlikely to significantly impact your privacy.
