üîß Troubleshooting Tips

Before contacting support, try these common fixes:

Game Not Loading

  • Close and reopen the app
  • Check your internet connection
  • Restart your device
  • Update to the latest version

Score Not Saving

  • Ensure you have an internet connection
  • Complete the game normally (don't force close)
  • Pull down on the leaderboard to refresh

Purchase Issues

  • Try the "Restore Purchases" option in Settings
  • Check your payment method in the app store
  • Restart the app after purchasing
