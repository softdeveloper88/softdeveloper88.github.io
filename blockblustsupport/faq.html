Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Blast Blocks

üéÆ Gameplay

How do I play Blast Blocks? ‚ñº

Blast Blocks is a puzzle game where you strategically place blocks on a grid. Your goal is to fill complete rows or columns to clear them and earn points. The game ends when you can't place any more blocks. Try to clear multiple lines at once for bonus points!

How do I earn more points? ‚ñº

Points are earned by clearing lines. Here are some tips to maximize your score:

  • Clear multiple lines at once for combo bonuses
  • Create chain reactions by setting up cascading clears
  • Keep the board clean to maintain flexibility
  • Plan ahead and think about where future blocks might fit
Can I undo a move? ‚ñº

Currently, moves cannot be undone once a block is placed. This adds to the strategic challenge of the game. Think carefully before placing each block!

What happens when I can't place any blocks? ‚ñº

When none of your available blocks can fit on the board, the game ends. Your score is saved and you can see how you rank on the leaderboard. You can always start a new game and try to beat your high score!

üèÜ Leaderboard

How does the leaderboard work? ‚ñº

The leaderboard shows players ranked by their total accumulated score across all games. Your scores from each game are added to your total. You can view Daily, Weekly, and All-Time leaderboards to compete with players around the world.

How do I change my display name? ‚ñº

Go to Settings ‚Üí Account and tap the edit icon next to your player name. Enter your new name and confirm. Your new name will appear on the leaderboard.

Why isn't my score appearing on the leaderboard? ‚ñº

Make sure you have an internet connection when playing. Scores are uploaded automatically after each game. If your score doesn't appear, try refreshing the leaderboard or check your connection.

Is my progress saved? ‚ñº

Yes! Your game progress, scores, and settings are automatically saved. Your data is stored both on your device and in the cloud, so you won't lose your progress.

Can I play offline? ‚ñº

Yes, you can play Blast Blocks offline! However, to submit scores to the leaderboard and access certain features, you'll need an internet connection. Scores achieved offline will be uploaded when you reconnect.

How do I restore my purchases? ‚ñº

Go to Settings ‚Üí Account ‚Üí Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same account you used to make the original purchases. This will restore any previously purchased items.

How do I delete my account/data? ‚ñº

To delete your account and all associated data, please contact us at support@blastblocks.com with your Player ID (found in Settings). We will process your request within 30 days.

üîä Audio & Settings

How do I turn off music/sounds? ‚ñº

Go to Settings ‚Üí Sound. You can toggle music and sound effects on/off independently, and adjust their volumes using the sliders.

How do I enable/disable vibration? ‚ñº

Go to Settings ‚Üí Gameplay ‚Üí Vibration. Toggle this setting on or off based on your preference.
